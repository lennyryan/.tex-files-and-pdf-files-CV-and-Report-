\section{Introduction}

 This report provides an overview of my personal portfolio website, developed to showcase my academic achievements and technical skills. The portfolio serves as a digital platform that enables employers and recruiters to learn more about me and access supporting documents.

\section{Motivation}
%\lipsum[2-4]
I was motivated to make this portfolio website to support job and internship applications and create networking opportunities. It will also serve as a repository for all my achievements and projects. This is bound to improve my professional visibility and demonstrate my technical capabilities. 


\section{Features}
The portfolio has the following key features:
\begin{itemize}
  \item About Section\\
  Relays a short Bio that pinpoints my interests and gives a small brief introduction of me and my career goals. 
  
  \item Education Section\\
  Displays my educational background and achievements in all levels of education.
  
  \item Project Section\\
  Showcases my completed projects and gives a small description of the projects, my input, and the technologies used in the project 
  
  \item Skills Section\\
  Shows my technical expertise across different fields and programming languages. Also displays my software proficiencies.
  \item Contact Section\\
  Provides channels I can be reached through for inquiries and networking opportunities. 
  
\end{itemize}

\section{Brand}
My personal portfolio brand resembles the science fiction genre, well known as cyberpunk. I chose it as my brand identity since it closely relates to advanced technologies in futuristic worlds and cities filled with artificial intelligence and a diverse cyberspace. It communicates a personality that is technology-oriented, innovative and professional. Its visuals offer a modern, user-focused and professional responsive interface.

\section{Weaknesses}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Complex Design Structure\\
    Some employers and recruiters prefer a minimalist portfolio design
    \item  Limited Real-World Experience\\
    No prior work experience and a lack of professional projects can make employers lack confidence in my skills.
    \item Lack of Testimonials\\ 
    No recommendations by a professor or lecturer lowers credibility.
    \item Performance Issues\\
    The webpage may be slow due to the high animations of the cyberpunk theme designs.
    
\end{enumerate}
